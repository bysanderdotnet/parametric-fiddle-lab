## Assembly Notes
- Strings thread through tailpiece slots, over bridge, fingerboard, nut, into pegbox
- Pegs insert from outside, friction-fit through tapered holes
- Fine tuners screw into tailpiece string holes
- Chinrest clamp-arm slides over rib edge; felt pad protects finish
- All printed parts use PLA (bambu_pla_basic profile)
